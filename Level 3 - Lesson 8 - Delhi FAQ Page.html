  • What is the best time to visit Delhi? +

    The best time to visit Delhi is during the cooler months from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

  • What is the best way to get around Delhi? +

    The best ways to get around Delhi are by using the Delhi Metro, which is efficient and covers most major areas, or by using ride-hailing apps like Uber and Ola. Auto-rickshaws and buses are also widely available.

  • What languages are spoken in Delhi? +

    Hindi is the most widely spoken language in Delhi. However, English is also widely understood and used, especially in tourist areas and by service providers.

  • What emergency numbers should I know while in Delhi? +

    Important emergency numbers include 112 for general emergencies, 100 for the police, 101 for the fire department, and 102 for ambulance services.

  • Do I need to tip in restaurants and hotels in Delhi? +

    Tipping is appreciated but not mandatory in Delhi. In restaurants, a tip of 5-10% of the bill is standard. In hotels, you can tip porters and housekeeping staff around ₹50-₹100.
